## Key Insights

### 1. Ride Demand
- Ride demand peaks during specific dates and hours indicating high traffic periods.

### 2. Vehicle Performance
- Prime Sedan and similar categories contribute the highest total ride distance.

### 3. Cancellations
- Majority of cancellations are customer-driven due to demand changes.
- Driver cancellations are mostly due to personal or car-related issues.

### 4. Revenue
- Digital payment methods contribute significantly to total revenue.

### 5. Ratings
- Higher driver ratings generally correspond to higher customer ratings.
